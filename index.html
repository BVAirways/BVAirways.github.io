Welcome to the home of British Airways Virtual

British Airways Virtual is an online virtual airline for gamers who thrive to perfect their skills at FSX & FS9 and would like to make it all the more real. While having the choice to choose between over 1000+ realistic routes to fly, and build your rank and earn virtual money, you get to realistically think about fuel consumption with respect to safety, economics and airline logistics. Among our pilots, are also professional ATC. We are proud to announce that we are also the sponsors of FSopen session called "Pro ATC at Gatwick" which offers professional ATC coverage, on a 24 hours/day server. We also fly on networks such as VATSIM and are private Network , we proudly train qualified pilots to fly with ATC on FSopen and at VATSIM level as well, with our VATSIM instructors and Flight instructors.

As a virtual airline we use smartCars to track and log our flights, they are monitored closely by our Flight Directors in order to ensure quality and safety, which is our pride as an airline. If you would like to be one step closer to your dreams...join us on teamspeak, server address: bavirtual.teamspeak3.com . You can click here to be connected automatically

We're delighted that you've found us! Use this site to discover more about our association and members. We meet regularly on our Teamspeak to take part in activities and enjoy each other's company. We were founded in 2012 to provide a place for young an old people to meet and learn new skills. Our association is made up of people of all ages who enjoy making new friends, meeting new ones and simply enjoy spending time together on our Teamspeak or at one of our events.
